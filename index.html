Our Services

Comprehensive diagnostic and interventional services using cutting-edge technology

Pregnancy / OB Ultrasound

Complete fetal monitoring, anomaly scans, growth scans, and doppler studies for expectant mothers with detailed reporting.

Abdominal Ultrasound

Detailed imaging of liver, gallbladder, kidneys, pancreas, spleen, and other abdominal organs for accurate diagnosis.

TVS / Pelvic Ultrasound

Transvaginal and pelvic sonography for gynecological evaluation, follicular monitoring, and pelvic pathology assessment.

Musculoskeletal USG

Specialized imaging for joints, tendons, ligaments, and soft tissue structures with dynamic assessment capabilities.

Echocardiography

2D Echo and color doppler studies for comprehensive cardiac evaluation and heart function assessment.

Intervention Procedures

USG-guided FNAC, biopsy, fluid aspiration, catheter placement, and other minimally invasive diagnostic interventions.
